# We are Nederland

The Town of Nederland is a unique and special place that residents proudly call home. Over time, this historic mining town has evolved into a thriving modern-day mountain community filled with people who love its historic and rustic character, breathtaking mountain scenery, proximity to outdoor recreation, and natural serenity. The purpose of this document is to chart a visionary path forward for the Town of Nederland, ensuring future growth and development aligns the community with its values. But before the community can fully know where to go, it is important to reflect on where the Town of Nederland has been.
